# Research

Unified skill for research and content extraction workflows.

## Workflow Routing

| Request Pattern | Route To |
|---|---|
| quick research, minor research | Workflows/QuickResearch.md |
| research, do research, investigate | Workflows/StandardResearch.md |
| extensive research, deep research | Workflows/ExtensiveResearch.md |
| deep investigation, map landscape | Workflows/DeepInvestigation.md |

## Research Rules

- Prefer source-backed findings with explicit citations.
- Distinguish confirmed facts from assumptions.
- Summarize key findings, open questions, and risks.
